About Me

Hey! My name is Samiyel D Frazier, im a sophomore at Barstow highschool (15 y/o), a published author, and the lead developer for my school's competitive robotics team. I'm interested in robotics, programming, art, and math. Im an avid learner, and constantly looking for ways to improve my work! Im always ready to learn or teach, and if you have any questions/advice feel free to contact me below!

Experience

Ive been in competitive robotics for over half a decade (thats about a 3rd of my life for those counting), and in that time ive learned so much. i was the sole programmer for a First Lego League robotics team in the 5th grade, which lasted up until i had to quit due to Covid-19. At the same time, I was also a member of honors band, playing trombone. I would play music for school wide events for hours on end... i dont much regret leaving band. those conditions were absolutely inhumane; we sounded good though!

Once we started coming out of lockdown and school was in full swing, i had started highschool. as a freshman, i joined Barstow High School's robotics club, which soon saw me as a core member of their team. I was about 3 meetings late from being a founding member, but i wont say im something im not.

Unfortunately, our first year was a bust. lots of technical difficulties and miscommunication lead to... unfavorable results. But this year, we're doing much better. I've published my first paper to the GCER archives (Global Conference on Educational Robotics), and im working on another!

Eden (Robotics Library)

Eden is a multi-platform repo used to interface with the KIPR Botball platform. Adam is a sub-branch, and the basis for Eve. Eve is an extension of adam, that offers the same functionality but with a roomba. My pride and joy, this repo has taken the most time out of my life compared to all other programs ive made. It was the reason i started learning github (hence the frankley horrible push and pull practices early on) and its over a year in the making. It's also a walk through memory lane, as the first time i had programmed in C (and C++) you see my code become more sophisticated as time goes on. Unforutunately, it does start on V4.0, earlier versions were stored on replit, which doesnt have a history.

Normal Polygon Generator

A short little program in Vex Vr that takes 2 parameters, sides and size, and makes the shape for you. One of my first programs in the language, and hopefully one of my last. I have a great disdain for block programming, i understand it has its uses but its so restricting. not to mention after working with it for years in middle school, it gets boring after a while. i know not all block languages are the same, but they all feel the same. I digress, I love bringing art math and robotics together in one cohesive manner, and this is an alright example of it.
